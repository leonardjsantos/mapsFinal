Injustice Alert: Mapping Anti-LGBTQ+ Legislation

Click on Your State and Call the Number to Demand Change!

by Leonard Santos

In wake of the wave of the many bills attacking the LGBTQ+ community in 2023, this site is dedicated to showing which bills are still active so that supporters and advocates have the tools they need to fight them.

All of the highlighted states have bills that are currently being debated on the Senate and House floors. Click on your state and call the number listed to advocate for change!

You can call the number, use this script to guide you:

"Hello, I am a constituent living in [STATE] and I'm calling about [BILL]. I ask that you vote to oppose this bill, as well as any other bill, that discriminates against the LGBTQ+ community. Thank you for your time and support."

Click on your state to learn about the anti-LGBTQ+ bills that you can advocate against!
